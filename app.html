    Innovation: Pankaj Sharma

    Storicool addresses the long standing challenge of content distributors and art studios. It is an innovative collaboration of AI, ML & MR to bring a breakthrough in content creation. AI is used to draw upon a large database of available assets and combine them in a way to automate creation though voice and text inputs.

    Read More

    • Vetturino

      Sense enhancement in vehicles. Vetturino is a product that sits as a live black box in your vehicles, managing a vehicle ecosystem in 4 paradigms; (a) Capturing driver performance characteristics using integrated sensors. (b) Capturing engine performance by interfacing with the vehicle ECM (up to 160+ parameters). (c) Accepting data from custom external sensors and cameras. (d) Allowing Control of car eco-system.

    • Pepper Powered by Entellio

      A humanoid robot with high empathetic quotients powered by ENTELLIO, Makers Labs in house conversational AI Framework. Peppers features include advanced language recognition,; Facial recognition and face tracking; Object recognition; Tablet for visual presentations and tactile feedback; Collision avoidance and many more. It can be used at various fronts such as front desk, navigation assistant, curator, etc.

    • X-Retail

      X-retail platform is a suite of unique solutions based on Next Gen technologies like iBeacons, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Mobile wallet payments, Analytics and Cloud which helps retailers track their customers In-Store behavior and preferences to provide them better quality of service; resulting in increased revenue and NPS.

    • MobiLytix

      Today, customer value management is gaining in importance. Comviva provides a portfolio of digital marketing solutions that help the marketer manage the end-to-end consumer life-cycle. The portfolio leverages big data-driven mobile analytics to uncover customer insights. This, in turn, ensures revenue growth and enhanced customer experience. MobiLytix enables large scale data fusion and advanced AI modelling capabilities, with decisioning models executed seamlessly in real time or batch mode through MobiLytix Real Time Marketing.

    • PayPLUS

      Across the world, financial services delivery and access is undergoing a sea change. Mobile is the new medium, access is the new paradigm. In both developing markets and the mature ones, this transformation now offers a unique opportunity to businesses to re-imagine their digital strategies in tune with an experience-centered world. Comviva is equipping businesses to seize this opportunity with unique, scalable and secure solutions. PayPLUS offers a unified interface for merchants and acquiring entities to manage all payment instruments, channels and technologies through a single interface. PayPLUS is geared to extract the most out of the recent policy changes with its merchant self-onboarding functionality which significantly lowers the onboarding cost and allows the merchant to start accepting payments by downloading the application.
